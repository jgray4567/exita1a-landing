The Problem

The average agent spends 65% of their working week on tasks that generate zero revenue. Manual research, compliance checklists, marketing creation, and follow-up scheduling are consuming the time that should be spent closing.

Time Hemorrhage

4–8 hours per CMA. 3–5 hours per week chasing showing feedback. 2–4 hours managing compliance paperwork per transaction. It compounds until your calendar owns you.

Revenue Bleed

Leads go cold in minutes, not hours. Every delayed response is a deal handed to your competitor. Industry data shows a 5-minute response yields 8x the contact rate of a 30-minute response.

Compliance Exposure

Document errors cause closing delays in 20–25% of transactions. A missed disclosure, an expired contingency, an unsigned addendum—any one of these can kill a deal or trigger a liability claim.
